FAQ

Quick answers, no fluff.

What is the 1% sold fee?
You pay 0 dollars upfront to list. If you sell your Tesla to a buyer who came from Only Used Tesla (a buyer who contacted you through our site), you pay a 1% sold fee based on the final sale price.
Example: $50,000 sale → $500 sold fee.
How long does my listing stay live?
The Basic Ad runs for 30 days.
Do I need a password?
Nope. We email you a secure sign‑in link so you can manage your ad without remembering passwords.
Is a history report included?
Not included by default. You can add AutoCheck or CARFAX for $20 if you want that trust badge.
What’s the video upgrade?
A 1‑minute max HD walk‑around (a simple 360°). It helps buyers feel confident and usually improves response quality.
Can you guarantee Facebook approval or group posting?
We can’t guarantee approvals — Facebook and group admins control that — but we’ll format the post cleanly and handle the posting service when you add it.
How do I contact you?
Use the contact link at the top (or reply to your listing email). Since this is a small business, you’ll hear back from the founder.
